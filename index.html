About Me!

Hello, my name is Shivani Ramdeo. I am 15 years old and attend Thomas A. Edison CTE high school. I am a dancer of Sanasani Dance Group. I have been dancing since I was 3 years old, but have only been a part of Sanasani for almost 6 years! Sanasani has been my favorite dance group as it's not just a dance class, it's a second family. We don't only dance, but we also go on vacation together and always plan hangouts when we have time. Every summer we have a beach day, pool day, waterpark day, concerts, amusement park day and a camping weekend. Every year we have our annual parties where we celebrate Cinco de Mayo, Christmas, Birthdays, and Thanksgiving. We act as though we've known each other our whole lives and are inseparable. We always say that God didn't make us actual siblings because we would be too much for our parents to handle under one roof.


A creative and divine way to express ones true self would be through the figurative language of dance.

About Sanasani

Sanasani Cultural Organization Inc, (SCOI) is a non for profit organization.Formed on June 19, 2012 by Annushka and Valentina, the group has grown immensely with 3 dance instructors (the newly adopted instructor Akash). Our sole purpose is to educate the students, parents and other members of the community about their religion, culture and heritage through selective dance forms, performing arts and other cultural and social activities; provided to the public at no cost. We promote and empower community peace & togetherness by having students, parents and other members from various religions, cultures, races, and ethnic backgrounds, working together as a family, which in turn cultivates new bonds of relationships, friendships & brotherhood within our community. Being one of the first charitable dancing organizations of Queens, New York, Sanasani Cultural Organization Inc. provides a platform for everyone to express themselves in the form of giving back to cultural arts, exceptional charitable work and community services. Advanced Intermediate Beginners

We have beginner, intermediate, and advance groups. Majority of performances are done by the intermediate groups and advance are done by special requests. Beginners are promoted to intermediate groups when all instructor agrees a student is ready. We accept boys and girls and each student is treated as their own individual. There are over Saturday morning class for the intermediate group, and a Saturday afternoon class for the advanced students.
